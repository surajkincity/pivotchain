How we work

Our Process

1.BrainStorm

We facilitate ideation workshops & conduct meetup sessions with your teams regularly to narrow down the key challenges faced by your business & build consensus on the expected outcome.

2.Research

As a data-driven organization, we conduct extensive technical research on the problem statement & the required solution, gathering key risks & challenges.

3.Prototype

Build an MVP (Minimal Viable Product) prototype as a Proof of concept of key challenges before committing to a larger solution.

4.Development

Once the MVP is built, we go through further iterations of incremental changes to arrive at the agreed result.

5.Deploy

We help you deploy (go-live) the solution using DevOps automation.

Career

At Pivotchain, we're building a world-class Analytics solutions helping our customers globally solve difficult data problem.

We offer you an inspiring and challenging atmosphere in which you can develop and flourish. We are looking for passionate Mathematicians, Data scientists and Programmers who have a wil to succeed under pressure.

Data scientist

  • You are good at Linear algebra, statistics & Probability skills, such as distributions, statistical testing, regression, etc
  • You take pride in your knowledge of design patterns, algorithms and data structures
  • You are comfortable processing, cleansing, and verifying the integrity of data used for analysis
  • You understand machine learning techniques and algorithms, such as k-NN, Naive Bayes, SVM, Decision Forests, CART, CHAID etc
  • You understand feature selection, model performance metrics, building and optimizing machine learning models
  • You are good at doing ad-hoc analysis and presenting results in a clear manner
  • You are good at creating automated anomaly detection systems and constant performance tracking
  • You can code comfortably in R & Python (NumPy, sklearn, xgboost)
  • You are experienced with data stores such as Mysql, MongoDB, Cassandra, HBase, Hive
  • You are experienced with data visualisation tools, such as Tableau, D3.js, GGplot, etc
  • You have a BS/MS degree in Computer science, Maths
  • Past experience with Deep Learning/NLP would be an advantage (although not necessary)

Backend Expert

  • You can think like an Architect & act like a Developer & Lead like a scrum master
  • You take pride in your knowledge of design patterns, algorithms and data structures
  • You are comfortable designing & developing scalable web applications
  • You are polyglot with at least 2 of following languages: Nodejs, Go, Python, Php, Java, Scala, Ruby
  • You are comfortable with databases: MySQL, MongoDB, Cassandra, Redis, SQL/JSON in general
  • You have worked on at least one of the cloud following cloud platforms: AWS, Azure or Bluemix
  • You are familiar with Micro service architecture & API design
  • You are familiar with JSON, XML data formats & REST, SOAP & RPC protocols (P2P would be added benefit)
  • Past experience with Fintech, P2P lending, Investment Banking or Insurance sectors would be an advantage (although not necessary)

Frontend expert

  • You are an expert in HTML/HTML5 (Semantic web), CSS/CSS3/Bootstrap
  • You are very strong in JavaScript (jQuery, AngularJS, Knockout, etc) skills.
  • You know how to handle compatibility quirks across browsers & create responsive designs
  • You are comfortable with PHP, Git, MYSQL, Angular.JS, AJAX, JSON, XML, WebSocket
  • You can work on Wireframing, UI/UX Design & Photoshop as needed
  • Any experience with D3js, Kibana, Tableau & other dashboard softwares would be an advantage (although not necessary)

DevOps Guru

  • You are an automation maniac
  • You are a Linux (Centos, Ubuntu) ninja
  • You are an open source evangelist
  • You have exceptional knowledge on encryption, security & networking
  • You have hands on cloud experience (At least one of the three: AWS, Azure or bluemix)
  • You are a talented engineer with mastery of github, Jenkins, Dockers, vagrant, ansible, Python, Powershell.
  • Understanding of what a blockchain is, would be an added advantage (although not necessary)

Blockchain expert

  • You are polyglot with at least 2 of following languages: (C, C++, Golang, haskell, scala & Python)
  • You have hands-on technical knowledge of Hyperledger, Ripple, Digital Asset, Multichain, Sidechain or Ethereum
  • You understand or have implemented smart contracts on Ethereum (Solidity) or Hyperledger (Chaincode)
  • You have a good understanding of encryption & cryptography (SHA/ECDSA, pub/priv key, hash functions, signatures and certificates)
  • You have an ability to quickly grasp complex business problems & can work on MVP as needed
  • You can assess and recommend changes to meet the business needs, based on clients’ needs.
  • You have a BS/MS degree in Computer science or Maths
  • Any experience in computational number theory would be an added advantage
  • Past implementation experience on blockchain or cryptography experience would be a def inite advantage
